Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

At Easy Shopping, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly, safely, and reliably. This Shipping Policy outlines our shipping process, delivery timelines, and related information.

🚚 FREE Delivery on all orders across India. No minimum order value required.

1. Order Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next working business day. You will receive an order confirmation via SMS or email once your payment is successfully processed.

2. Delivery Timelines

Estimated delivery times after order dispatch:

LocationEstimated Delivery Time
Metro Cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata)2–4 business days
Tier 2 & Tier 3 Cities4–6 business days
Remote / Rural Areas6–10 business days

These are estimated timelines. Actual delivery may vary due to courier partner operations, weather conditions, strikes, or other unforeseen circumstances.

3. Shipping Coverage

We currently ship to most PIN codes across India. To verify if we deliver to your area, please enter your PIN code on the product page. We do not currently offer international shipping.

4. Shipping Charges

Easy Shopping offers free shipping on all orders, regardless of the order value. There are no hidden delivery charges. The final price at checkout is the total amount you pay.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via SMS or email. You can use this tracking number on the courier partner's website to monitor your delivery status. Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to become active after dispatch.

You can also track your order directly from the Orders section in the Easy Shopping app or website.

6. Delivery Attempts

Our courier partners will attempt delivery up to 3 times. If delivery is unsuccessful due to an incorrect address, unavailability of the recipient, or refusal to accept the package, the order may be returned to us. In such cases, a re-delivery fee may apply, or a refund will be issued after deducting applicable charges.

7. Packaging

All orders are securely packed to prevent damage during transit. Fragile or delicate items receive additional protective packaging. If you receive a damaged package, please photograph it before opening and contact us at support@easyshopping.in within 48 hours of delivery.

8. Delayed Deliveries

While we strive to meet estimated delivery dates, delays may occasionally occur due to:

  • High order volumes during festive seasons or sales
  • Natural disasters, floods, or extreme weather events
  • Courier partner operational issues
  • Government-imposed restrictions or curfews

In case of significant delays, our customer support team will proactively reach out to you. You may also contact us to check the status of your order.

9. Incorrect or Incomplete Address

Please ensure that the delivery address provided at checkout is complete and accurate, including the flat/house number, street, area, city, state, and PIN code. Easy Shopping is not responsible for failed or delayed deliveries caused by incorrect address information provided by the customer.
