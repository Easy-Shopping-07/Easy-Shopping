Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

At Easy Shopping, we strive to ensure that you are fully satisfied with every purchase. If you are not happy with your order, we offer a transparent and hassle-free return and refund process as described below.

✅ We offer a 7-day return window from the date of delivery for eligible products.

1. Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The return request must be raised within 7 days of delivery.
  • The item must be unused, unwashed, and in its original condition with all tags intact.
  • The product must be in its original packaging.
  • A valid proof of purchase (order ID or receipt) must be provided.
  • Items that are damaged due to misuse, improper handling, or normal wear and tear are not eligible for return.

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following categories of products are not eligible for return or refund:

  • Personal care and beauty products once opened (e.g., skincare, cosmetics)
  • Innerwear, socks, and swimwear for hygiene reasons
  • Digital products or downloadable goods
  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page
  • Products that have been altered, customised, or personalised

3. How to Raise a Return Request

To initiate a return:

  • Go to the Orders section in the Easy Shopping app or website.
  • Select the order and click "Request Return".
  • Select the reason for return and submit the request.
  • Our customer support team will review and respond within 24–48 hours.

Alternatively, you can email us at support@easyshopping.in with your order ID and reason for return.

4. Return Pickup

Once your return request is approved, we will arrange a pickup from your delivery address at no additional cost to you. Please ensure the item is packed securely before the pickup. In some cases, we may ask you to self-ship the item to our warehouse, in which case shipping charges will be reimbursed.

5. Refund Process

After we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process the refund within the following timelines:

  • UPI / Bank Transfer: Refund processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund reflected within 7–10 business days depending on your bank.
  • Store Credit / Wallet: Instant credit, available for use on the next order.

You will receive an email or SMS notification once the refund is initiated.

6. Damaged or Wrong Item Received

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Share clear photographs of the product and the packaging to support@easyshopping.in. In such cases, we will offer a full refund or replacement at no cost to you.

7. Order Cancellation

You may cancel an order before it is shipped. To cancel, go to the Orders section and click "Cancel Order". Once an order has been shipped, cancellation is not possible — you will need to follow the return process after delivery. Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3–5 business days.
